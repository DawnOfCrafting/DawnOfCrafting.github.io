Description

We are 2 brothers making a unique iOS game focused on crafting mechanics.

We were frustrated by all the crappy game companies who didn't give a shit. So we decided to make one which did give a shit.

We care about our players, their actions, inputs, thoughts, ideas. We care about our game and its playability.
